Cendol Perak

A refreshing dessert made with fresh coconut milk, palm sugar, and rice flour jelly for a sweet finish. Perfect for hot days, this traditional Perak dessert combines natural sweetness with a hint of saltiness from the fresh coconut milk. Served chilled, it's a must-try for visitors to the region.
